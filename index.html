About Us

Where passion meets technology — a space where ideas become projects and ambitions turn into achievements.

We Are Scorpio

Scorpio Club is a student tech community founded in 2017 at Abou Bekr Belkaid University of Tlemcen. We bring together passionate minds in cybersecurity, IT, and innovation to create, learn, and inspire. At Scorpio, we believe in the power of technology to shape the future — and we are building it, one idea at a time.

We empower students through hands-on workshops, inspiring conferences, and real-world projects. If you believe passion makes the difference, then you belong with us!

Departments Us

Social Media

Our Social Media department is dedicated to managing the club’s online presence by creating engaging and innovative content across various platforms. The team builds strong relationships with the audience, increases brand awareness, and keeps the community updated with the latest events, initiatives, and opportunities.

Design

The Design department plays a crucial role in crafting creative visuals, designing user interfaces, and maintaining the visual identity of the club. From event posters and social media posts to UI/UX designs for applications and websites, the team ensures that all content is both professional and visually captivating.

Secretariat General

The Secretariat General in the club oversees all administrative functions including the organization of meetings, documentation of minutes, and archiving of important records. It acts as a central point for communication between committees, ensuring that operations are coordinated and that strategic goals are achieved in a smooth, organized, and timely manner.

Human Resources

The Human Resources (HR) department is responsible for managing recruitment processes, onboarding new members, and fostering a positive and productive environment within the club. The HR team continuously works on member engagement, professional development, and internal communication to ensure that all members are motivated, aligned, and growing together towards the club’s vision.
